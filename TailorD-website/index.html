How does it work?

Download the Extension

Start by going to our page on the chrome web store and adding the extension. You will go through the quick process of creating an account.

Enter a few details

Once the extension is added you enter a few details and then you'll be prompted with a QR code.

Scan your body with your phone

Scan that QR code on your phone and complete the 2 minute scanning process in somewhat form fitting clothing.

Start shopping

Return to the extension when promted and you are ready to start shopping on any online clothing store.
